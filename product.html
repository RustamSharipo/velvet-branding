Peggis. Brand & packaging design for tomato paste

Brand: Peggis

Product: Tomato paste

Peggis is a family-owned brand of tomato preserves from Uzbekistan. All their tomatoes come from biological farming on the plantations of the Fergana Valley. Client asked us to create unique packaging for their product. Our goal was to create laconic design separate them on a shelf with different colors

Art director: Jamal Akbarov

Graphic designers: Jamal Akbarov, Khojiakbar Rakhimov
